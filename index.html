Dr Njoaka Cyprain "A Family Man And Pilar Of The Community"

Here's a time line of Dr. Njoaka's life till present day:

  • 1967 - This is the year of birth
  • 1990 - The year he lost his dad
  • Background - Come from a poor family of 7 siblings 2 of which are death
  • Status - Married to his lovely wife of 35years
  • Education - He is a certified nurse and Anaesthesist.
  • Home-Town - He was born and breaded in Kumbo town in the North-west of Cameroon .
  • Children - He is a father of 5, 4 girls and the eldest, a boy.
  • Profession - He has saved over 200+ lives in his long medical career.
  • Person - He is a social and kind person always involved in community betterment.
  • Accomplishment - He is a well decorated Anaethesist and has in the pass years step out from his normal day job working with the Saint Elizabeth Catholic Hospital Kumbo to establish his own family clinic. He continues to help the commmunity in any little way he can.
"From the day i came in to this world till present, i have been proud to call you my daddy. I am the lady i am today, because of the man you are. You are my greatest inspiration and role model. This world is lucky to have you. Keep saving and touching lives positively. I love you dad. "
